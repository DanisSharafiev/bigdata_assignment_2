Yan Zibei (, born 12 October 1995) is a Chinese swimmer. He competed in the men's 100 metre breaststroke event at the 2016 Summer Olympics. He won a silver medal when he competed in the 4x100m mixed medley relay at the 2020 Summer Olympics.
