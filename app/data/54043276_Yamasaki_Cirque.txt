300px|right|thumb|Mount Tateyama and Yamasaki Cirque Yamasaki Cirque is a cirque located on the North-West side of Oyama peak of Mount Tateyama, Hida Mountains, Japan. ==Outlines== It is 400 metres wide, and its length is 600 m. It is one of the first cirques discovered in Japan by geographer Naomasa Yamasaki (1870–1929), in 1905. It was named after Yamasaki in 1942. In 1945, it was assigned as natural monument by national government as "Yamasaki Cirque of Tateyama".
