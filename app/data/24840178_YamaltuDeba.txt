Yamaltu/Deba is a Local Government Area of Gombe State, Nigeria. Its headquarters is in the town of Deba (or Deba Habe), at the southeastern part of the state capital, Gombe. Its inhabitants are dominantly Tera and Fulani people. The southern part of Lake Dadin Kowa lies within the area. It has an area of 1,981 km and a population of 255,248 according to 2006 census.
