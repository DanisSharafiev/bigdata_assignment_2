Yasnaya Polyana () is a rural locality (a village) in Kiprevskoye Rural Settlement, Kirzhachsky District, Vladimir Oblast, Russia. There is 1 street. == Geography == Yasnaya Polyana is located on the Shorna River, 26 km northeast of Kirzhach (the district's administrative centre) by road.
