"You're Gettin' a Little Too Smart" is a song written by Abrim Tilmon and performed by the Detroit Emeralds. It reached number 10 on the R&B; chart and just missed the Billboard Hot 100 in 1973, reaching number 101.The Detroit Emeralds, "You're Gettin' a Little Too Smart" chart positions Retrieved January 17, 2016. The song was featured on their 1973 album, I'm in Love with You.
