Yahritza y Su Esencia are an American trio from Washington State's Yakima Valley who specialize in Mexican regional music and ranchera. The band includes three siblings: singer and songwriter Yahritza Martínez, Armando (Mando) Martínez playing the requinto, and Jairo Martínez playing bajoloche (a form of bass). For the week of April 9, 2022, their song "Soy el Único" appeared on the Billboard Global 200. Yahritza wrote the song when she was only fourteen.
