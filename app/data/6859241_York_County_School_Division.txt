The York County School Division or YCSD is a school division (school district) in York County, Virginia, United States. The division consists of approximately 12,750 students in 19 schools, of which there are 10 elementary schools, 4 middle schools, 4 high schools, and 1 charter school. The division employs about 1,050 instructional staff members and over 730 support staff members. == Governance == York County School Division is run by a five-member School Board of elected officials, one from each of the county's five election districts.
