Yi Baidi (; born 10 October 1993 in Shenzhen) is a Chinese footballer who plays for Jiangsu Yancheng Dingli in the China League Two. ==Club career== Yi Baidi joined Hangzhou Greentown youth team system from Shenzhen Yantian Sports School.
