Your Universe is the debut album by former Rivermaya frontman, Rico Blanco, released in 2008. A vinyl version of the album were released on July 28, 2019. ==Overview== After Annoncing his departure on Rivermaya, Rico went on a year- long leave from the local music scene and toured the US and Europe.
